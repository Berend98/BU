Acedemy for Digital Entertainment
The Academy for Digital Entertainment comprises the bachelor’s program of ‘International Media and Entertainment Management’ and ‘International Game Architecture and Design’, as well as the master’s programs of ‘Media Innovation’ and ‘Game Technology’. For more information about the Academy for Digital Entertainment, visit the website.

Breda University of Applied Sciences
Breda University is located in the south of the Netherlands, in the city of Breda. BU offers bachelor’s and master’s programmes in Games, Media, Hotel, Facility, Logistics, Built Environment, Tourism and Leisure & Events. For more information about Breda University of Applied Sciences, visit the website.
