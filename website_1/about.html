About us

We are 7 students currently studying International Media and Entertainment Management (IMEM) at the Breda University of Applied Sciences. For the course Mobile Application Development the assignment was to create an hidden object game located in school.

Within seven weeks we developed this application named 'BUrn'. We based the application on the new name of the school and the new campus that will be finished by next year and were inspired by the worldwie trend 'The floor is lava'.
